SWASABHARATH

swatantrya sarvodaya Bharata

India is a land of fighters, we know the stories of Extremists and Moderates who had fought for this country and have achieved eternity. During the period of Independence, many people were involved in the act of freedom but we remember only a few. Swasabharath is an organization, they visit a certain locality and conducts a program to felicitate those people who have dedicated their lives to the country. The program will have songs that contain the stories of those people and their life achievements and there will be few speeches about their struggle and life. All these stories and songs and the entire program is lead by Sadananda Gowda. Sadananda Gowda is the grandson of Sardar Shri Mallikarjuna Gowda who is a well-known freedom fighter and a man with a kind heart and has sacrificed his entire life to the country. Initially, this organization is started with the admiration of Sardar Shri Mallikarhuna Gowda and sarvodaya movement chronicles but later on Mr.Sadanand and his friend Mr.D R Hegade thought it will be good if we admire and honor the other freedom fighters as well in the locality so that they feel overwhelmed and recognized for their sacrifices. Our first program was conducted in Anantapur later on it was conducted in Anavatti, Yennekoppa,Haveri,Shivamogga, and many other places. All we require is your blessings and please do visit our programs and its date and timings will be posted on this website on the home page.
